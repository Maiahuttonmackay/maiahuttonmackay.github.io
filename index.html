Do you need help choosing what to wear?

Let Carrie Br(ai)dshaw chose for you!

Introducing What Would Carrie Wear?, an AI-powered bot trained to generate fashion-forward outfits inspired by Carrie Bradshaw's iconic looks from Sex and the City. Drawing from her bold, eclectic style, the bot curates unique ensembles by blending your own wardrobe with vintage pieces and daring accessories. Whether you're after a signature tutu-and-heels combo or a chic power suit, What Would Carrie Wear recreates the character's fearless fashion choices while offering modern twists. Ideal for anyone looking to channel their inner Carrie, the bot delivers personalized outfit recommendations that capture her spirit of individuality and bold creativity. Simply upload photos of the clothes you want to wear for your fabulous event, select the item you would like Carrie to finalise for you, and press send to recieve Ms Br(ai)dshaw's style advice!
